## Universal Viral Elements

### **Opening Hooks (First 3 seconds)**
1. **Pattern Interrupt**: Unexpected statement or visual
2. **Bold Claim**: Strong numerical or outcome statement
3. **Question Hook**: Engaging viewer curiosity
4. **Visual Hook**: Striking imagery or contrast

### **Content Structure**
1. **Hook** (0-3s): Capture attention immediately
2. **Problem** (3-8s): Establish pain point or curiosity
3. **Solution Preview** (8-15s): Tease the value coming
4. **Value Delivery** (15-50s): Core content/information
5. **Social Proof** (50-55s): Credibility and results
6. **Call to Action** (55-60s): Engagement request

### **Engagement Mechanics**
- **Comment Triggers**: "Comment X for Y"
- **Follow Hooks**: "Follow for more Z"
- **Share Prompts**: "Share if you agree"
- **Save Triggers**: "Save this for later"

## Rapid Production Workflow

### **5-Minute Production Cycle**
1. **Script Selection** (30 seconds): Choose template and customize
2. **Voice Recording** (2 minutes): Record narration
3. **Visual Assembly** (2 minutes): Add visuals and text overlays
4. **Final Review** (30 seconds): Quality check and optimization

### **Batch Production Strategy**
- Record 10 voice tracks in one session
- Create visual asset libraries
- Prepare template variations
- Schedule content in advance
